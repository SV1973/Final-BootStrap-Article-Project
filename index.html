2. CYBER SECURITY

Every Big organization from Tech Companies to Governmental Bodies generate a large amount of valuable Data. Hence it is very important for them to keep it safe from unauthorized access and attacks.

This is where the Cybersecurity comes in.

Cybersecurity is protection of computer systems and networks from the theft of or damage to their hardware, software and electronic data, as well as from the disruption of the services they provide.

Since the no. of users and the devices are increasing, providing the cybersecurity to everyone is becoming more challenging than ever.

Leak of digital data can cause major threat to mankind. The most common forms of digital threat also known as Cyber Crimes are as follows :-

  • Phishing
  • Identity theft
  • Hacking
  • Online Harassment

After getting a look on above list that can take place due to lack of Cybersecurity, It is more wiser decision to accept that

Cyber secure is less of a technology and more of a responsibility.

Since the cyber attackers are getting more skilled and innovative, the companies are increasing the security budget year by year to thrawt malacious threats.

Companies like IBM, Deloitte, Amazon, J.P.Morgan, Hackerone, Fireeye, Cisco, Blackberry, Avast, are apparently in search of good skilled Cybersecurity candidates.

3. CLOUD COMPUTING

Cloud Computing is one of the emerging technology as it solves the modern problem of Big Tech Giants and other organizations as well.

Cloud Computing in simple terms is defined as using the remote servers instead of local servers or personal computers for storing, managing and processing data.

Cloud computing has only 3 General models :-

  • Software as a service (SaaS)
  • Platform as a service (PaaS)
  • Infrastructure as a service (IaaS)

Now Cloud Computing also includes has :-

  • Everything as a service (XaaS)
  • Disaster Recovery as a service (DRaaS)
  • Communication as a service (CaaS)
  • Network as a service (NaaS)

It is predicted that 83% of enterprise workloads will be in the cloud by 2020. Half of the IT professionals firmly believe that AI and ML will paly a role in Cloud Computing.

Also, it can converse in natural language and helps in decision making by understanding the complexities of Big Data.

The below advantages will surely make more sense to moving towards to cloud computing :-

  • Reduced IT costs.
  • Scalability.
  • 24 x 7 availability
  • Secure and Efficient
  • Flexibility of work practices

7. DevOps

DevOps is a set of practices that combines software development (Dev) and IT operations (Ops).

It is a philosophy that can help software organizations :-

  • Innovate Faster
  • More Responsive to Business Needs
  • Promotes Better Collaboration between Developers and Operations
  • Better Software Quality
  • More Frequent Releases

It aims to shorten the systems development life cycle and provide continuous delivery with high software quality.

It came as a solution to keep the workflow of software development as smooth as possible.

The following are the benefits of having a DevOps oriented team offers :-

  • Increase the Rate of Software Delivery
  • Improves Company's Time to Market
  • Maintains Better Business Focus

Not just that DevOps has made it's way in AI through Data Ops.

With the increasing use of microservices, DevOps continues to pay with its way into our big players like Amazon, Netflix, NASA, Facebook, Capgemini and Dell which are a few of the existing pioneers in this industry.

10. BLOCKCHAIN

Bitcoin the crypto currency and only word that strikes your mind when you hear the word "Blockchain".

Blockchain is a list of records called blogs that are linked together using cryptography.

Blockchain can be categorized as a data structure that holds transactional records and while warranting

  • security
  • transparency
  • decentralization

Blockchain offers security that is useful in many other ways. In the simplest of terms, blockchain can be described as data you can only add to, not take away from or change.

Hence the term “chain” because you’re making a chain of data. Not being able to change the previous blocks is what makes it so secure.

Each transaction on a blockchain is encrypted with a digital signature that validates its authenticity. This makes the dta stored on the blockchain tamper-proof and secure.

IT professionals are already working on blockchain to provide a secure underlying infrastructure to many aspects of out digital life

Companies extensively working on this technology include IBM, mythical games, salt lending and gemini.
